About

This web app could be used when a bunch of people visit a restaurant and they have to split the bill per head at the end. As seen in most of the restaurants, taxes are added separately at the end which is a particular percentage (say 14.6%) of each item. In that case, if both veg and non-vegetarians are sharing the bill then it would be fair to divide the taxes equally too. So this app would help to calculate the exact amount one owes in no time and without much calculations.
